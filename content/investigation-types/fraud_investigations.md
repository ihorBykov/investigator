Fraud investigations focus on identifying financial abuse, laundering activity, social engineering operations, and illicit monetization workflows across traditional and blockchain-enabled ecosystems.  
  
These investigations frequently involve multi-chain transaction tracing, victim interaction analysis, operational attribution, and cash-out exposure assessment.  
  
---  
  
## Common Investigation Areas  
  
- pig butchering scams  
- insider fraud  
- laundering operations  
- social engineering campaigns  
- exchange exposure analysis  
- structured cash-out behavior  
- intermediary wallet tracing  
  
---  
  
## Operational Characteristics  
  
Observed fraud operations frequently demonstrate:  
- layered transaction structures  
- operational wallet separation  
- cross-chain movement  
- intermediary laundering wallets  
- centralized exchange exposure  
- social engineering infrastructure
