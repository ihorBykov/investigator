> Investigations involving long-term financial grooming scams utilizing cryptocurrency payment infrastructure.  
  
---  
  
## Overview  
  
Pig butchering investigations focus on:  
- tracing victim payment flows  
- identifying scam infrastructure  
- mapping laundering pathways  
- correlating operational artifacts  
- identifying operational entities  
  
These scams commonly involve:  
- social engineering  
- fake investment platforms  
- stablecoin transfers  
- layered laundering activity  
  
---  
  
## Common Investigative Objectives  
  
- identify operational wallets  
- trace victim funds  
- identify exchange exposure  
- map laundering pathways  
- identify infrastructure overlap  
  
---  
  
## Common Data Sources  
  
### Blockchain Data  
- victim transaction flows  
- stablecoin transfers  
- intermediary wallets  
- exchange exposure  
  
### Infrastructure Sources  
- fake investment platforms  
- phishing infrastructure  
- operational domains  
- communication platforms  
  
### OSINT Sources  
- social profiles  
- operational aliases  
- reused contact data  
- public scam reports
