Blockchain investigations involve tracing cryptocurrency transactions, identifying operational wallet relationships, analyzing laundering behavior, and supporting attribution through financial intelligence workflows.  
  
These investigations combine blockchain analytics, OSINT, operational correlation, and infrastructure analysis to reconstruct illicit financial activity.  
  
---  
  
## Common Analytical Areas  
  
- wallet attribution  
- transaction tracing  
- exchange exposure analysis  
- layering and structuring detection  
- peel chain analysis  
- laundering workflows  
- cross-chain movement analysis  
  
---  
  
## Investigative Objectives  
  
- identify operational wallets  
- trace illicit fund movement  
- support attribution investigations  
- identify laundering infrastructure  
- detect exchange cash-out exposure  
- reconstruct financial timelines
