> Investigations involving ransomware operations, affiliate ecosystems, payment infrastructure, and actor attribution.  
  
---  
  
## Overview  
  
Ransomware investigations combine:  
- blockchain tracing  
- infrastructure analysis  
- malware ecosystem research  
- actor attribution  
- operational intelligence  
  
Modern ransomware operations often involve:  
- affiliate programs  
- leak sites  
- cryptocurrency laundering  
- infrastructure compartmentalization  
  
---  
  
## Common Investigative Objectives  
  
- identify ransomware operators  
- trace ransom payments  
- analyze affiliate activity  
- map infrastructure components  
- identify monetization channels  
  
---  
  
## Common Data Sources  
  
### Blockchain Intelligence  
- ransom wallets  
- laundering chains  
- exchange exposure  
- intermediary wallets  
  
### Infrastructure Artifacts  
- leak sites  
- C2 infrastructure  
- malware samples  
- hosting artifacts  
  
### Threat Intelligence  
- actor communications  
- TTP overlap  
- affiliate activity  
- operational infrastructure reuse  
  
---  
  
## Common Investigative Workflow  
  
```text  
Ransomware Event  
↓  
Wallet Identification  
↓  
Blockchain Tracing  
↓  
Infrastructure Correlation  
↓  
Threat Actor Attribution  
```
