---  
  
## Overview  
  
Insider fraud investigations focus on:  
- attribution of internal actors  
- tracing unauthorized transactions  
- identifying laundering pathways  
- correlating operational behavior  
- reconstructing fraud timelines  
  
These investigations often involve:  
- privileged access abuse  
- unauthorized fund transfers  
- operational concealment attempts  
- staged transaction activity  
  
---  
  
## Common Investigative Objectives  
  
- identify responsible individuals  
- reconstruct transaction flows  
- identify laundering mechanisms  
- correlate operational access  
- assess financial exposure  
  
---  
  
## Common Data Sources  
  
### Blockchain Data  
- operational wallets  
- exchange deposits  
- intermediary wallets  
- laundering chains  
  
### Internal Artifacts  
- authentication logs  
- transaction systems  
- access records  
- administrative activity  
  
### OSINT & Intelligence  
- behavioral indicators  
- linked identities  
- reused infrastructure  
- communication artifacts  
  
---  
  
## Common Investigative Workflow  
  
```text  
Fraudulent Transaction  
↓  
Wallet Attribution  
↓  
Transaction Tracing  
↓  
Operational Correlation  
↓  
Identity Attribution  
```  
  
---  
## Common Challenges  
  
- use of intermediary wallets  
- layered laundering activity  
- insider operational knowledge  
- partial logging visibility
